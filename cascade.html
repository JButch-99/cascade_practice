    About Us

    Here at The Second Chance Sanctuary, we're dedicated to the wellbeing of all of our furry friends! From the small rodent to the mighty horse! We'll take care of any of our furry or scaly guests, be it reptilian, mammals, or avian! Our mission statements are as followed:

  • The wellbeing and safety of animals!
  • To ensure we can bring our animals to happy homes!
  • To connect the community with knowledge on animal care!
